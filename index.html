Crystals Collector

*You will be given a random number at the start of the game*

*There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your crystal counter*

*You win the game by matching your crystal counter to the random number. You lose the game if your crystal counter goes above the random number*

*Each time the game starts, the values ascribed to the crystals will change*
